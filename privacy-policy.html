Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy of DIGITY Media FZCO

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how DIGITY Media FZCO ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal data. Protecting your information is a significant concern for us, and we commit to respecting your privacy in accordance with the laws of the United Arab Emirates (UAE).

2. Collection of Personal Data We collect the following information from our users:

● Names

● Addresses

● Email addresses

● Telephone numbers

● IP addresses

● Cookies

● Usage data

These data are collected to provide you with targeted marketing communications, newsletters, enhanced customer service, and an optimized user experience on our website.

3. Disclosure of Data

Your personal data may be shared with our partner companies and other business units within our corporate group to provide the services mentioned above. All recipients of this data are obligated to maintain the confidentiality and security of your data.

4. Data Protection Measures To protect your personal data, we implement advanced security technologies and procedures, including:

● Data transmission encryption

● Access controls

● Regular security audits

These measures are designed to prevent unauthorized access, unauthorized modification, and unlawful disclosure of your data.

5. User Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data and to object to data processing. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer, Tyrone Flint Brösel, at info@digitymedia.de.

6. Data Retention

Your data will be stored as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected or until you revoke your consent.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in locations in Germany, the USA, and the Philippines. We ensure appropriate safeguards through the use of standard contractual clauses and other security mechanisms.

8. Consent

Your consent to process your data is obtained through various mechanisms, including your agreement via Calendly and confirmation during newsletter signup.

9. Compliance with Local Laws

Our data protection practices are compliant with local UAE laws, and we commit to continuously reviewing and adjusting our policies according to legal requirements.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about the processing of your personal data, you can always contact us: Tyrone Flint Brösel, DIGITY Media FZCO, DDP Building A2, Silicon Oasis, Dubai.
